The educational Games Publishers Association of Kenya (EGPAK)
is a non-profit membership organization serving all individuals and
SMEs(Small and medium Enterprises) who have a common interest in educational games

Our individuals and SMEs(Small and medium Enterprises) are exposed to networks,
partnerships and resources that enable them to thrive in both personal
and proffessional levels.

Our Objectives:

    To suppport members and interest groups produce,develop,publish and market digital educational games
    To streghthen the digitalgames development and marketing sector
    To advise and share information on digital eduvational games
    To create marketable, research-based educational games
    To assist teachers on the use of digital educational games
    To recruit members consisting of game developers
    To remain a non-profit and non-political association

Mission

To create a community of all individuals, Small and Medium Enterprises(SMEs)
and organizations dealing with educational games.

Vission

Make learning easy and fun for everyone in the world.
